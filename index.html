ESG Meets Web 3.0

C02 Labs is focused on developing Environmental, Social, and Governance (ESG) & Corporate Social Responsibility tools. We fill the gap between Web 3.0 and any entity that wishes to follow the respective compliance requirements while ensuring that companies can quickly and easily post their data in a transparent manner, thus bringing more awareness to the space.

Our Mission

"The decarbonizing of the global economy is going to create the greatest investment opportunity of our lifetime.‚Äù

- Larry Fink, CEO of BlackRock

C02 Labs is an R&D tech company that fuses Environmental, Social and Corporate Governance (ESG) and Corporate Social Responsibility (CSR) with scalable low energy technologies. We are technology agnostic and partner with blockchain companies to create solutions that have tangible impacts on the fight against climate change and improve social equality.

Our motivation is to simplify ESG/CSR reporting which is often complex and time consuming and thus allow for a larger audience to implement ESG/CSR standards and regulations which are based on chain technologies.

Our Approach

  • Planet

    We make sure that our service only relies on eco-friendly components from the top to bottom.

  • Process

    Entities simply drag'n'drop the ESG compliance files onto our service and the data gets automatically published.

  • Technology

    C02 labs relies on blockchain technology to publish this ESG compliance data in a transparent manner.
